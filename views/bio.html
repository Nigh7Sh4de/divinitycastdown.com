Name Leo Halajda
Position Drums
Description I'm from Poland and came to Toronto in 2000. Playing Drums since 1986. I've played in a couple of bands, but it was not going in the direction that I wanted it to. I felt I was being pushed to the edge and was ready to quit drumming. Thanks to my wife, Chris, who turned the basement into a rehearsal place. She has been my biggest supporter and would never let me give up, because of her I am still playing drums. I met Rob and finally someone who, like me - seriously dedicated and a professional musician with similar taste in music; we work well together! I'm a huge cat lover and hope all those cats that we have in the house, they are not going to scratch my face off for the loud noise that we make every rehearsal.
Influences Hate, Decapitated, Vader, Behemoth, Amon Amarth, Cannibal Corpse, Pantera, Fear Factory, Obituary, Havok, Soulfly, and many more.
Gear 8 Piece Tama Rockstar, Zildjian Cymbals, Pearl Hardware
Name Marius Necula
Position Bass
Description Originally from Romania, I came to Canada in 2006. I've played guitar for over 20 years and I enjoyed playing with a few blues, classic rock and metal bands back in Romania. After being away from the stage presence for over 10 years, I decided to go back and try the Canadian experience. I met Rob and Leo who were looking for a bass player to complete the band (Divinity Cast Down) lineup and after few rehearsals/auditions, I've been officially asked to join the band.
Influences My Dying Bride, Cannibal Corpse, Elend, Beyond Creation, F. Schubert, J. S. Bach, Faceless, Opeth, Depeche Mode, Nile, and many more.
Current Listenings My Dying Bride, Beyond Creation, F. Schubert, Elend, Faceless
